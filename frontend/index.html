Our Mission

"Go therefore and make disciples of all nations..." - Matthew 28:19

Our church is dedicated to preaching the Gospel, serving the community, and providing a place of worship for all.

Orphanage Home & Feeding Program

Our church operates an Orphanage Home, providing shelter, education, and love to children in need.

Every week, we organize a Food Distribution Program, offering meals to underprivileged families and the homeless.

Orphanage Home

We provide a safe home for children who have lost their parents, ensuring they receive education, healthcare, and spiritual guidance.

Weekly Food Distribution

Every week, our volunteers prepare and distribute food to poor families, bringing hope to those struggling to find meals.
